Lasagna

Lasagna done in 30 minutes or less

Home made dish that works perfectly as comfy food for you and your family

Ingredients

  • Grinded Meat
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • All purpose flour
  • Lasagna Pasta

Steps

  1. Add some olive oil to the pan and simmer your meat with chopped onion, garlic and tomatoes
  2. On low heat, add butter to a pan, 100g of flour and 250ml of milk gently while stirring
  3. Preheat the oven at 250Â°c
  4. Start adding the Lasagna Pasta as layers to a glass baking dish, alternating with the tomatoe/meat sauce and the Bechamel sauce
  5. Add chese to the top and cook for about 10-15 mins with the oven closed
  6. Serve and enjoy
